📲 Add to Home Screen
🧭Safari
🌐Chrome
1
Open MoneyFlow in Safari
Make sure you are using Safari. Chrome and other browsers do not support installation as an app on iPhone.
2
Tap the Share button
At the bottom center you'll find the icon with an arrow pointing up. Tap it to open the share menu.
3
Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
In the menu that appears from the bottom, scroll the options to find this item with the square "+" icon. Tap it.
4
Confirm with "Add"
A preview will appear with the app name. Tap "Add" in the top right corner to complete the installation.
On iOS, apps added via Safari run full screen without the browser bar, just like a native app.
1
Open MoneyFlow in Chrome
On Android, use Chrome for the best experience. On iPhone, Safari is preferred, but Chrome works too.
2
Tap the three dots in the top right
Chrome's main menu opens by tapping "⋮" in the top right corner of the screen.
3
Select "Add to Home Screen"
In the dropdown menu, look for "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app". On Android a banner may also appear automatically at the bottom.
4
Tap "Add" to confirm
A confirmation popup will appear. Tap "Add" and the MoneyFlow icon will appear on your Home Screen.
On Android with Chrome, the app is installed as a PWA and appears in your app list with its own icon and splash screen.
